Direct and Indirect Questions

 

Make questions to the answers below

 

1-)Who works at Itaú bank?

R: Cathy works at Itaú bank.

 

2-)Where does Sérgio live?

R: Sérgio lives in Miami.

 

3-)Who bought a new car?

R: My parents bought a new car.

 

4-)What did Robert break?

R: Robert broke the vase.

 

5-)Who speaks four languages?

R: Susan speaks four languages.

 

6-)Who reads book? (Who/read/book)

R: People do.

 

7-)Who gave you flowers? (Who/give/you flowers)

R: Peter did.

 

8-)What did Sam see? (What/see)

R: Sam saw an horrible accident.

 

9-)Who lives in that house? (Who/live/in that house)

R: The Smith do.
