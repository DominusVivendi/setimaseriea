Reinforcement Exercise – If Clause (I)

 

 

1-) If Charles eats (eat) too much, he will have (have) a stomachache.

 

2-) Pedro won’t go (not/go) to the beach, if it rains (rain).

 

3-) If I don’t study (not/study), I won’t pass (not/pass) on my exams.

 

4-) If he is (be) busy, I will call (call) him later.

 

5-) My parents will buy (buy) a new car if they earn (earn) much money.

 

6-) Susan will watch (watch) the film if she goes (go) to shopping mall.

 

7-) If Carlos doesn’t get (not/get) good grades, he won’t go (not/go) the exchange program.

 

8-) If  Frances enjoys (enjoy) the meeting, he will come (come) back in other occasions.

 

9-) We will travel (travel)  to Miami if we have (have) enough money.

 

10-) If she has (have) on free time, she will write (write) her diary.
